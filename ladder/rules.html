Ladder Competition Rules


The object of the ladder is to get as high a ranking as possible by the end of the season. The overall winner will be the person at the top of the ladder at the end of the season. As this is a mixed ladder there will be both a womens' and men's winner.

1 You can challenge a team 1 or 2 places above you in the ladder.
2 Matches can be played at any time, on any court (except during club night). Please book an Embassy court if using.
3 Matches will be 2 sets long. If you are 1-1 a 7 point tie break will determine the winner.
4 If you are the challenger and win, you will swap places with the team you have beaten. If the challenger loses, there is no change.
5 Results (Date of Match, Name(s) of Winning Player/Team, Name(s) of Opponent Team/Player, Score) are reported to Joel at the AATC Tennis Ladder whatsapp group.
6 You can play an unlimited number of matches.
7 If you do not play a match in a two week period, you will drop one position. This will happen even if you are out of the country or injured (it just mixes up who can play who!)
